Fleur Edwards is a Vice President in Technology at Nomura in New York, where she works on corporate architecture initiatives. She received a Bachelor of Commerce (Honours) from the University of Melbourne and is currently pursuing a BSc in Computer Science from the University of London, Goldsmiths. She is a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) Charterholder and is a registered financial professional with FINRA Series 7,24 and 63 licenses. In addition to her work in technology and finance, she was also named as a Future Leaders Role Model on the Involve Outstanding 2024 list for her work in inclusion.
